News and Events

    What are teachers doing to keep their skills sharp while we get through the current virus crisis? Much more than you think.

    While many parents have become temporary teachers of sorts, Maryland school kids are lucky to have a few teachers taking the extra step and instructing their students online.

    Sandi Brower is one eager to keep things fresh. With more than 23 years of experience, Brower is a Reading and Math Intervention Teacher at Middletown Elementary School in Middletown, MD.

    Recently, after hearing about The Unstoppable Jimmy, she asked if she could read our book to her students. We proudly agreed as our book demonstrates how children cope with tragedy while their parents often have trouble.

    Thank you, Sandi Brower for your desire to share our book with your students.
