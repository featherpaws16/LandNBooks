Who Are We?

A u t h o r s :  L i a m  O' B r i e n  &  N o r e e n  O' B r i e n

Authors Liam and Noreen O'Brien make their home in Richmond, Virginia. Liam is sophmore at the College of Engineering at Virginia Commonwealth University while Noreen works as a writer and editor with VCU as well. Both Liam and Noreen are accomplished photographers. Liam is a prolific poet and loves online gaming when he's not working in the computer lab on an engineering project. He's also an avid reader. Noreen enjoys ceramic arts and creating art & literature classes for youngsters and teens. They both love expressing themselves through writing. Very excited to be starting the process of having their first book published, working with the most amazing illustrator and publicist, Liam and Noreen are lookingforward to along relationship with Brandylane Publishers.

P u b l i c i s t :  M a r i e  P i c h a s k e

Marie glows and radiates excitement in all she does. First and foremost, she believes in our book and without that, why bother? Her perseverance and dedication is what drew us to her in the first place, not to mention her connections.Savvy and smart, witty and kind, Marie binds us all together.

I l l u s t r a t o r :  J e s s i c a  B e r g

Our families go back many years and we have watched Jessica grow into quite an accomplished artist. Our earliest memory of her talent was when she created an "outer space" themed rocking chair for Liam when he was a child.   We still have this today - you just cannot toss "priceless" stuff like that.  Simply put, she has the unique ability to capture expectations and get them on paper, resulting in beautifully crafted and poignant illustrations.  We are so incredibly grateful and lucky to have her on this journey with us. Please follow Jessica's fablous art on her Facebook page and Instagram, both Jessicabergfinearts.
